About

The project vision is to transform the project management experience at the Debug Divas™ software start-up through the novel application Motion™.

An innovative and cutting-edge project management tool aspiring to foster productivity amongst Debug Divas™ developers who are implementing Scrum practices.

As a multi-user application designed to facilitate the creation and management of a Product Backlog and Sprint board, Motion™ aims to provide a unified way in which to effortlessly document such Scrum practices.
