About

The project vision is to transform the project management experience at the Debug DivasTM software start-up through the novel application MotionTM,

an innovative and cutting-edge project management tool aspiring to foster productivity amongst Debug DivasTM developers who are implementing Scrum practices.

As a multi-user application designed to facilitate the creation and management of a Product Backlog and Sprint board, MotionTM aims to provide a unified way in which to

effortlessly document such Scrum practices.
